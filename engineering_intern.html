Mechanical Engineering Intern

Background

This experience came as a surprise to me- I didn't plan on working over the summer, but when I received the offer to work as an intern for Rieck Services, I decided to seize the opportunity. Rieck Services is one of the largest and most successful mechanical contractors in the Dayton, Ohio area, providing services that range from HVAC repairs to designing and building new structures. When I applied to attend the University of Cincinnati, I was originally trying to decide whether I wanted to go into mechanical or electrical engineering. Although I am now completely confident in my choice of major, I was still excited to experience a totally different field of engineering, especially one I've always been interested in!

The Experience

My main responsibilities were drafting proposals and price estimates for various jobs, performing HVAC-related calculations such as heating and cooling loads, and creating and/or modifying schematics and drawings in AutoCAD MEP 2021. I also attended numerous job sites and assisted with surveying and assessing them. I had zero experience with AutoCAD before working for Rieck, so I decided to compile some of the schematics and drawings that I worked on to show some of my progress. You can see them below! They are in order of when I made them (the oldest ones appear first) to better show any improvements.
