About Me

Hello! My name is Katy Hildebrant. Welcome to my portfolio! I am currently a fourth-year student at the University of Cincinnati pursuing my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering and a minor in computer science. I was born and raised in Dayton, Ohio but fell in love with Cincinnati after visiting my older sister while she attended UC! On campus, I am highly-involved with the University of Cincinnati chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). I have served as the president of the organization since summer 2022 and am extremely excited to see what we can accomplish in the coming semesters. You can learn more about us at https://ieee.uc.edu ! I was also the director for MakeUC, the annual fall hackathon hosted by IEEE at UC, in 2022 and 2023. You can learn more about that at https://makeuc.io ! When I'm not in class, I love to work on electronics projects, read, watch movies, and spend time outside. Additionally, I will be returning for my fourth and fifth co-ops with HII at Wright Patterson Air Force Base in January 2024.
