About Me

Hello! My name is Katy Hildebrant. Welcome to my portfolio! I am currently a third-year student at the University of Cincinnati pursuing my bachelor's degree in electrical engineering and a minor in computer science. I was born and raised in Dayton, Ohio but have have always loved Cincinnati. After visiting my older sister at UC, who graduated in 2019, I fell in love with everything about it. I was only in sixth grade at the time, but I knew this is where I wanted to go one day! On campus, I am highly-involved with the University of Cincinnati chapter of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). I have served as the president of the organization for the last year and am extremely excited to see what we can accomplish in the coming semesters. I am also the director for MakeUC, the hackathon hosted annually by IEEE at UC. You can learn more about that at https://makeuc.io if you like! When I'm not in class, I love to work on electronics projects, read, watch movies, and spend time outside. I also recently completed my second co-op with Huntington Ingalls Industries at Wright Patterson Air Force Base in December and will be returning to work for them again later this month.
