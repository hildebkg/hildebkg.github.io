University Honors Program Portfolio

The University Honors Program (UHP) is comprised of the top 7% of undergraduate students at the University of Cincinnati. Students in UHP are responsible for participating in at least five "honors experiences" during their time at UC. This portfolio is a compilation of my experiences and reflections so far.

Bearcats Academy Mentor

My experience working as a mentor with the Bearcats Academy program for Cincinnati high school students.

Mechanical Engineering Intern

My experience working as a mechanical engineering intern for Rieck Services in Dayton, Ohio.

MakeUC Director

My experience directing MakeUC, the annual hackathon hosted by the University of Cincinnati chapter of IEEE.

Honors Portfolio Upgrade

My experience redesigning my honors portfolio into the one you see now!
