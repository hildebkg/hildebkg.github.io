Year in Review: 2021-2022

Prompt: What specific experiences (honors or otherwise) in the past year have had the most impact on your personal, academic, or professional goals and life trajectory? Which future experiences will further encourage this growth? Please articulate specific academic and/or professional goals for the next year.

This past year, I was able to form much more concrete professional goals thanks to my first co-op experience. I was very lucky to work for Huntington Ingalls Industries at Wright Patterson Air Force Base for the spring semester and will be returning there for my next co-op in the fall. One of the reasons this experience was so impactful to my professional goals was because it exposed me to many different projects. Before this experience, I was confident in my major and my interests, but I was not sure how to pinpoint which interests and specific areas of study were best suited to me. While I worked on several projects throughout my co-op, I had two larger, long-term projects that were quite different from one another and that helped me discover what I enjoyed most. One project was a desktop application that I developed; another was a circuit board that I helped design. Both projects were very different in the sense that they required completely different knowledge and skills. I was grateful to work on both because they provided me with the time and practice necessary to better determine what I enjoyed doing most. The entire experience on co-op increased my confidence in my field and introduced me to several new skills, some of which I had never even heard of! Many skills, like soldering, were so enjoyable that I invested time outside of work to continue improving them. This experience as a whole has helped me form one specific professional goal in particular: I want to stay with the company for future co-ops and then work there full-time after graduation! Future experiences that will encourage this growth include, of course, my upcoming fall co-op as well as any other co-op positions I may have with the company. The facility in which I work has two main divisions: testing and systems development. I was heavily involved in systems development this past semester, so I plan to be more involved in testing in the fall. This change will expose me to even more situations and skills and will help me decide what exactly I want to do as a life-long career.

While I was able to set some important goals this year, I have a few more that I would like to work towards next year. One academic goal I have is to take my first honors seminar! There is a seminar called Pinball Design and Programming that I have been trying to take since I was a freshman, and my goal is to make room in my schedule to take it in 2023. I am extremely excited, both for the class content and for the professor! Another academic goal that I have for next year is to get more directly involved in one of my favorite classes by being a teaching assistant or a tutor. I have really enjoyed a lot of my classes so far, and I would love to help other people be successful with them. I would really like to tutor and/ or help teach because it helps me keep the concepts fresh in my mind. I also find it really interesting and helpful when people ask me questions about topics, because they often think of things in ways that I don't. In summary, I learned and grew a lot this past year, and I will use this progress to guide and inspire my growth for next year!
